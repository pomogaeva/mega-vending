About

Unbeatable Vending Quality
and Customer Service

Mega Vending is a family-owned and operated business that was founded in 1997 by experienced Vending Machine & Playground operators.

Mega Vending has developed a reputation for providing innovative products and generating significant additional income for customers whilst improving the overall F&B and shopping experience.

Due to the size of our product portfolio, we can provide our customers with an unparalleled choice of retail entertainment equipment.

Mega Vending distributes and operates vending machines & playground equipment manufactured by Beaver Machine Corp., Jolly Roger, Unis and ITL, American changer Corp, as well as the confectionery manufactured by Oak Leaf & Dubble Bubble.
